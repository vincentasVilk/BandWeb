Coldplay are a British rock band formed in London in 1996.
The band comprises vocalist, rhythm guitarist and pianist Chris Martin, lead guitarist Jonny Buckland, bassist Guy Berryman, drummer Will Champion, and creative director Phil Harvey.
They met at University and began playing music together from 1996 to 1998, first calling themselves Pectoralz and then Starfish.
